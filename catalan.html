Information About Catalan Numbers

Catalan numbers are a sequence of natural numbers that have various applications in combinatorial mathematics, particularly in the context of counting various combinatorial objects.

Properties of Catalan numbers:

  • They appear in various counting problems, such as counting the number of ways to form different types of parentheses sequences.
  • They satisfy a recursive formula, making them useful in dynamic programming algorithms.
