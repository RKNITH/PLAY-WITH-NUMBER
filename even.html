Information About Even Numbers

Even numbers are integers that can be evenly divided by 2. Examples of even numbers include 2, 4, 6, 8, 10, etc.

Properties of even numbers:

  • They are divisible by 2.
  • Their units digit is always one of the digits: 0, 2, 4, 6, or 8.
  • The sum of two even numbers is always an even number.
  • The product of two even numbers is always an even number.
