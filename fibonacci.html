Information About Fibonacci Numbers

The Fibonacci sequence is a series of numbers where each number is the sum of the two preceding ones, usually starting with 0 and 1. The sequence is named after the Italian mathematician Fibonacci.

Properties of Fibonacci numbers:

  • The sequence starts with 0 and 1.
  • Each subsequent number is the sum of the previous two.
  • Examples of the sequence: 0, 1, 1, 2, 3, 5, 8, 13, 21, etc.
