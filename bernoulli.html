Information About Bernoulli Numbers

Bernoulli numbers are a sequence of rational numbers which are deeply connected with number theory and mathematical analysis.

Properties of Bernoulli numbers:

  • They appear in the Taylor series expansions of the tangent and hyperbolic tangent functions.
  • They are used in the calculation of the sums of powers of integers.
