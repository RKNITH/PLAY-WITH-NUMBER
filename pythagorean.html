Information About Pythagorean Triples

Pythagorean triples are sets of three positive integers (a, b, c) such that \(a^2 + b^2 = c^2\).

Properties of Pythagorean triples:

  • They are fundamental in mathematics and have applications in various fields, including geometry and number theory.
  • Examples include (3, 4, 5), (5, 12, 13), and (8, 15, 17).
