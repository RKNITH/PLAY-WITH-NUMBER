Information About Centered Polygonal Numbers

Centered polygonal numbers are a sequence of figurate numbers formed by a central dot surrounded by a polygonal arrangement of dots.

Properties of centered polygonal numbers:

  • They are generated by adding layers of dots around a central dot, forming various polygonal shapes.
  • Each centered polygonal number corresponds to a specific polygonal shape, such as triangles, squares, pentagons, etc.
