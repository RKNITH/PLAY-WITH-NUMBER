Information About Gaussian Numbers

Gaussian numbers are complex numbers of the form a + bi, where a and b are integers and i is the imaginary unit (i^2 = -1).

Properties of Gaussian numbers:

  • They extend the concept of numbers from the real number line to the complex plane.
  • They are widely used in various branches of mathematics, including number theory and signal processing.
