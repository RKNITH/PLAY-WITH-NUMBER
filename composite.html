Information About Composite Numbers

Composite numbers are positive integers that have at least one positive divisor other than one or itself. In other words, a composite number is any positive integer greater than one that is not a prime number.

Properties of composite numbers:

  • They have more than two factors.
  • Examples of composite numbers include 4, 6, 8, 9, 10, 12, etc.
