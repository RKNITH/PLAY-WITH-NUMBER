Information About Prime Numbers

Prime numbers are natural numbers greater than 1 that have no positive divisors other than 1 and themselves. Examples of prime numbers include 2, 3, 5, 7, 11, etc.

Properties of prime numbers:

  • They have exactly two distinct positive divisors: 1 and the number itself.
  • The number 2 is the only even prime number.
  • Prime numbers are used in various fields such as cryptography.
